Thank you for supporting Loom over the past five years.

At the start of a project, the twists and turns ahead are unmapped. We are pleased to announce that Loom has been acquired by Blackstorm Labs. As part of this, the Loom SDK online presence is being wound down. Don't worry - all existing licenses are valid and will remain in force. The publicly available source code will remain public under its existing license.

The Loom SDK has been The Engine Company's focus since inception, but the time has come to move on. The Engine Company will continue to serve existing clients.

You can contact support AT theengine DOT co with any Loom related inquiries.

- Ben & the Loom SDK Team
